🔐 Raven VPN Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Raven VPN respects your privacy. We collect only what is necessary to provide secure internet access.

Data We Collect:
• Phone numbers (hashed, never stored in plaintext)
• Payment transaction IDs (for audit purposes)
• Anonymous visit counters (device type, region)

Data We DO NOT Collect:
• Browsing history or visited websites
• IP addresses of your activities
• Personal files or messages

Data Retention:
• Voucher codes and session records are deleted after expiry.
• Failed attempt logs are auto-purged after 30 days.

Third-Party Services:
Pesapal (payments) and Yoola (SMS) process data securely under their own privacy policies.
